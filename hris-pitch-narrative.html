The Problem

HR teams at MSEs and SMEs are buried in spreadsheets, email chains, and paper forms. Onboarding, payroll, leave requests, compliance tracking — all done by hand. It's slow, error-prone, and burns out the people who should be focused on hiring great talent and building culture.

⚡

Your Solution

An HRIS built specifically for growing businesses — automating the repetitive HR workflows that eat up time every single day. From onboarding to offboarding, leave to compliance, your platform replaces manual effort with smart, configurable automation.

Pain Points
What you're replacing — slide by slide
📋
Manual onboarding processes
▾
New hires wait days for paperwork. HR chases signatures across email threads. Documents get lost or filed incorrectly. Your platform replaces this with automated onboarding flows — from offer letter to day-one access, all in one place.
Pitch line: "We cut onboarding time from 3 days to 3 hours."
📅
Leave & attendance tracked in spreadsheets
▾
HR managers still maintain manual leave registers in Excel. Errors, disputes, and missed approvals are common. Your platform introduces self-service leave management with auto-approvals, calendar sync, and real-time balance tracking.
Pitch line: "We eliminate the spreadsheet that every HR team still uses."
📊
Performance reviews done via email
▾
Review cycles are chaotic — chasing managers for feedback, compiling responses manually, missing deadlines. Your platform automates review scheduling, reminders, and aggregation so HR can focus on outcomes, not logistics.
Pitch line: "Performance reviews that actually get completed — on time."
⚖️
Compliance risk flying under the radar
▾
Labour law compliance is complex and changes often. SMEs especially lack the resources to track it. Your platform flags compliance gaps, automates required documentation, and keeps audit trails — reducing legal exposure significantly.
Pitch line: "We protect businesses from the compliance risks they don't even know they have."
